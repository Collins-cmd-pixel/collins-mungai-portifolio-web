Hey, I'm Collins 👋

I'm a Front-End Developer and Graphics Designer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I specialize in crafting digital experiences that are not only functional but genuinely beautiful — because I believe great design and great code are inseparable.

My journey started with a passion for how things look, which naturally evolved into understanding how they work. Today I sit at that intersection — turning design ideas into living, breathing interfaces that users love to interact with.

I specialize in responsive web development, UI/UX design, and brand identity creation. Whether it's building a pixel-perfect landing page or crafting a brand's visual language from scratch, I bring the same level of craft and care to every project.
