Frequently asked

How long does a typical project take?
A landing page usually takes 3–5 days. A full website takes 1–3 weeks. UI/UX design projects vary. I always give a clear timeline before starting.
Do you work remotely?
Yes, 100%. I work with clients across Kenya and internationally. Communication is primarily via email, WhatsApp, and video calls.
What's your payment process?
I require a 50% deposit to begin work, and the remaining 50% upon project delivery. I accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and PayPal.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes — all projects include 2–3 rounds of revisions. I want you to love the final result, so I work closely with you throughout the process.
Can you redesign an existing website?
Absolutely. Website redesigns are one of my core services. I analyze what's working, what isn't, and create a better version that aligns with your goals.
